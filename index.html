Your Home in the heart of Hot Springs

Come relax in the mountains at the Red Bridge Cottage. This charming 1930's river rock home is in the heart of Hot Springs! Walk out the front door, and you'll be right across the street from the famous natural Hot Springs Spa that gives the town its namesake. Take a few steps in the other direction and you're in the quaint downtown. Walk a little further to the campground, and if you're lucky, you'll catch one of the many fun festivals. Cross the bridge and go for a hike on Lovers Leap trail or the Appalachian Trail which runs right through downtown. Or maybe you walked out the back door, and you found a huge back yard, whimsical patio, and a fire pit right on Spring Creek.

Hot Springs Resort & Spa

Located at the nexus of the French Broad River and lovely Spring Creek, Hot Springs Resort & Spa offers private, outdoor, natural spring hot tubs in an idyllic setting. Supplied by a natural hot mineral water spring that continuoulsly flows, you can soak and relax your cares away, with a romantic partner or a group of close friends or family members. Add a massage to complete your relaxation and rejuvenation experience.

White Water Rafting

Hot Springs Rafting is a local family-owned company offering full- and half-day rafting adventures, as well as funyak and tube rentals on the French Broad River. Let us fine-tune your river adventure, including guided and unguided adventures, with or without lunch, and shuttle service to and from the put-in and take-out areas. Enjoy the outdoors, get wet, and have a barrel (or a raft) of fun!
