Welcome to The Red Bridge Cottage
Your home in the heart of Hot Springs.

Book Now

Come relax in the mountains at the Red Bridge Cottage. This charming 1930’s river rock home is in the heart of Hot Springs! Walk out the front door, and you’ll be right across the street from the famous natural Hot Springs Spa that gives the town its namesake. Take a few steps in the other direction and you’re in the quaint downtown. Walk a little further to the campground, and if you’re lucky, you’ll catch one of the many fun festivals. Cross the bridge and go for a hike on Lovers Leap trail or the Appalachian Trail which runs right through downtown. Or maybe you walked out the back door, and you found a huge back yard, whimsical patio, and a fire pit right on Spring Creek. Relax, fish, play, sip, eat, and sleep to the rippling water. Enjoy your meals outside on the patio or at the picnic table down by the river. You really can park the car for your entire vacation if you like! Yet, a short car ride away opens you to all kinds of adventure: hiking, white water rafting, kayaking, tubing, fishing, mountain biking, ziplining, and horseback trail rides.

On the main floor of the house, you’ll find a cozy living room with gas fireplace, an open dining room off the fully stocked kitchen, a large family room also with a gas fireplace, laundry room, and full bath. The sectional couch in the family room folds out to a full size bed offering flexible sleeping arrangements.

Upstairs you’ll find another full bath and 2 comfortable bedrooms each with king beds. The larger bedroom also has a twin bed. House has 50” Smart TV, DVD player, & WiFi internet access. Charcoal grill is provided.

Up for a day trip? Asheville with its quirky artist scene, great downtown, multitude of craft breweries and the Biltmore Estate are only 45 minutes away.
